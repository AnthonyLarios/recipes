Ingredients:
 - 2 slices white bread
 - 1 slice cheddar cheese
 - 1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
 1. Heat a flat pan a little under medium heat.

 2. Spread 1/2 tablespoon of butter on one slice of bread. Place on the pan to melt
    the buttered side.

 3. Place slice of cheese and bread on the cooking slice of bread. Spread other 1/2
    tablespoon on other slice of bread.

 4. Cook both side of sandwhich until golden-brown.
